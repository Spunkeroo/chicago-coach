What Relationship Coaching Covers

  • Communication skills: Active listening, non-violent communication, expressing needs without blame. The single most impactful skill for any relationship
  • Conflict resolution: Learning to fight fair, repair after arguments, and address underlying issues instead of surface symptoms
  • Attachment styles: Understanding how your attachment style (anxious, avoidant, secure, disorganized) affects your relationship patterns
  • Boundaries: Setting and maintaining healthy boundaries with partners, family, and friends. Boundary work transforms relationships more than almost any other skill
  • Intimacy: Emotional and physical intimacy building. Addressing mismatched needs and creating deeper connection

How to Choose a Relationship Coach in Chicago

  1. Check credentials: Look for ICF certification (ACC, PCC, or MCC) plus specific relationship coaching training (Gottman Method, Relationship Coaching Institute, etc.)
  2. Specialty match: A dating coach is different from a couples coach. Choose someone who specializes in your specific situation
  3. Book a discovery call: Most coaches offer a free 15-30 minute consultation. Use this to assess chemistry and approach
  4. Both partners must buy in: For couples coaching, both people need to want to be there. Coaching does not work when one person is dragged in reluctantly
  5. Ask about their approach: Homework-based coaches produce faster results than talk-only approaches. Look for coaches who assign between-session exercises

Frequently Asked Questions

Is relationship coaching effective?

Yes. The International Coaching Federation reports that 73% of coaching clients report improved relationships. Couples coaching is most effective when both partners are committed to the process and complete homework between sessions.

How much does relationship coaching cost in Chicago?

Expect $150-$400 per session in Chicago. Couples coaching tends to cost more than individual coaching. Package deals (8-12 sessions) typically offer 10-20% savings over per-session pricing.

Can a relationship coach save my marriage?

Coaching is most effective for couples who are functional but want to improve. If you are in crisis, start with a licensed couples therapist. Many couples use both: therapy for healing, coaching for building new skills.

Do I need my partner to attend relationship coaching?

Not necessarily. Individual relationship coaching helps you develop communication skills, understand your patterns, and make changes that positively affect all your relationships — even if your partner never attends.

How long does relationship coaching take?

Most clients see improvement in 4-6 sessions (1-2 months). Significant relationship transformation typically requires 3-6 months of consistent coaching with homework practice between sessions.
